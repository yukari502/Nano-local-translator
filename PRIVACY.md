# Privacy Policy

**Nano Local Translator** is built with privacy as its core principle. 

## Data Collection
We **do not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, browsing history, or website content to any external servers. 

## Local Processing
All translations are processed **100% locally** on your device using Chrome's built-in Translator API. The text on the web pages you visit never leaves your computer.

## Permissions
- **`<all_urls>` / `activeTab`**: Required strictly to read the text of the current webpage you want to translate and to display the translated text back onto the page.
- **`storage`**: Used solely to save your local UI preferences (such as target language, display mode, and TTS settings).
- **`tts`**: Used locally to speak selected text aloud without sending audio data to third-party servers.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or cloud APIs.

If you have any questions, you can open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/yukari502/Nano-local-translator).
